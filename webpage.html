About Us

Why We Started

HerSaathi was founded with a single goal in mind: to empower women by offering them a platform for safety and support. Witnessing the daily challenges women face, we knew we had to take action. We wanted to build more than a website—we wanted to create a movement, where technology meets community to make safer spaces for women everywhere.

What We Offer

Through HerSaathi, we bring together powerful features that allow users to access immediate assistance, connect with supportive peers, and learn essential safety skills. Our website includes real-time alerts, safe space mapping, emergency contacts, and a network of like-minded women who believe in supporting each other. Every feature is designed to give women peace of mind and a sense of community.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every woman feels empowered, safe, and confident in her surroundings. At HerSaathi, we’re committed to fostering a supportive environment where women can access the help they need, connect with others, and grow through shared experiences and resources. We believe that by building stronger communities, we can help create safer spaces for everyone.

Our Mission

  • Enhance Immediate Safety: By offering real-time alerts and mapping safe spaces, we ensure women can quickly reach out for help and find secure locations.
  • Foster Community Support: We believe that strength lies in unity. HerSaathi is built to connect women, helping them support one another through virtual support groups, resources, and local networking.
  • Promote Empowerment: Beyond immediate safety, we provide resources for self-defense, legal rights, mental health, and more. We want every woman to feel confident and equipped to handle any situation.
  • Innovate with Purpose: We continuously refine and expand our platform, inspired by the voices and experiences of our users. Our goal is to create a space that evolves with the needs of our community, always focused on safety, inclusivity, and empowerment.

By combining technology with community-driven insights, we aim to set new standards for women’s safety and empowerment. At HerSaathi, we’re committed to making every woman’s journey safer and stronger, one connection at a time.
